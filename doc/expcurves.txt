Exponential ADSR curves used in maucisynth

Attack:

s = 4
b = 1 / (1 - e^(-s))
f(x) = b * (1 - e^(-s * x))
x = 0..1

    optimization:
    t = -s * x
    f(t) = b * (1 - e^t)
         
         
    
Decay:

s = 4
b = 1 / (1 - e^(-s))
f(x) = 1 - (1 - sustain) * b * (1 - e^(-s * x))

Release:

s = 4
b = 1 / (1 - e^(-s))
f(x) = prevAmplitude * (1 - b * (1 - e^(-s * x))
